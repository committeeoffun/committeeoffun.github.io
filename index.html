About us

The Committee of Fun was founded by a bunch of PhD students in Science Park who, desperate to escape the jealous embrace of Academia, decided to start some sort of organization that would break down the wall separating their alienated and research-bound comrades from the free world.
Our goal is to organize events, and, more imporantly, to provide a place for people to organize their own events, in and around Amsterdam.
