About us

The Committee of Fun was founded by a bunch of PhD students in Science Park who, desperate to escape the jealous embrace of Academia, decided to start an organization dedicated to going out and having adventures and generally living life as it should be lived.

Our other goal is to do as little work as possible to achieve this first goal. As such we have created a mailing list to allow members to launch their own trips.
